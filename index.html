Recipe for PB and J

A simple and delicius sandwich. A true lunch classic

The finish product

Ingrediens

  • Sliced bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly or jam

instructions

  1. lay out to slices of bread
  2. spread peanut butter evenly across one side of the bread
  3. do the same with the jelly
  4. press the to slices of bread together
